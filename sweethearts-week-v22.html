Sweetheart's Week

Sigma Phi Epsilon — Kansas Eta · Sept 1–6, 2025

Panhellenic × SigEp

All Events at a Glance

Welcome! SigEp is excited to host a week of events designed to bring people together, relax, and create lasting memories. Join the ones that match your vibe, or experience the full week with us!

Questions? Message the VP of Programming at 620-352-0324 or ask a brother.

This Week

Monday — Sweethearts & S'mores: Outdoor Movie Night

Time: 8:00–11:00pm Location: Sigma Phi Epsilon

Ease into Sweetheart’s Week with a casual outdoor movie night at the SigEp house. Watch a good movie, enjoy s’mores by the fire, and mingle with brothers, sorority members, and guests in a laid-back atmosphere.

S’mores supplies provided. Bring a blanket if it gets chilly.

Tuesday — Sweethearts Petals & Positivity

Time: 2:00–4:00pm Location: SigEp Chapter Hall

Join SigEp in pairing up to design your own bouquet and take it home, or gift it to someone special.

All supplies included.

Wednesday — Sweethearts Karaoke Night

Time: 6:00–8:00pm Location: SigEp Basement

Join SigEp for a karaoke night! Take the mic for solo, duet, or group karaoke and sing your heart out with friends.

Light snack provided.

Thursday — Sweethearts Volleyball Night (Tournament)

Time: 7:00–10:00pm Location: SigEp Sand Volleyball Court

Join SigEp for a tournament-style sand volleyball night where teams of brothers and Panhellenic members compete for a prize!

Winners take home a prize!

Team signup: Sign up here

Saturday — Fall for the Sweethearts: Meadowlark Farm Apple Jam

Time: 3:30–6:00pm Location: Meadowlark Farm Orchard & Cidery, 11249 SW 160th St, Rose Hill, KS 67133, USA

We’re closing out Sweetheart Week with an autumn outing to Meadowlark Farm’s Apple Jam. Join SigEp for apple‑picking, fresh cider, yard games, and live music.
